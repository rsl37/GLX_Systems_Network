## Key Themes & Insights

### Algorithmic Stablecoin Failures
Citations 203-222 provide extensive analysis of the Terra/Luna collapse, offering critical lessons on the fragility of algorithmic stabilization mechanisms. These sources include academic papers, regulatory analyses, and industry post-mortems documenting the systemic risks inherent in under-collateralized designs.

### Tokenomics as System Architecture
Citations 223-245 establish tokenomics as a fundamental design discipline, highlighting common failure patterns including poor distribution models, inadequate incentive alignment, and lack of sustainable value accrual mechanisms.

### Stablecoin Payment Infrastructure Evolution
Citations 246-265 document the emergence of stablecoins as critical payment rails, with particular focus on institutional adoption, banking integration, and the potential for multi-trillion dollar market expansion predicted by industry leaders like Arthur Hayes.

### Regulatory & Market Maturation
Citations 266-295 reflect the regulatory evolution of stablecoins from experimental instruments to potentially systemically important financial infrastructure, with analysis from Federal Reserve researchers, global banking institutions, and legal frameworks.
