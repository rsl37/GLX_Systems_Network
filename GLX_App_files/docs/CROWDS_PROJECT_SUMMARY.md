## üìû Questions and Clarifications

### Common Questions

**Q: Is CROWDS operational?**
A: No. CROWDS exists as comprehensive design specifications. Smart contracts, AI systems, and reserve backing have not been built or deployed.

**Q: Can I use/buy CROWDS tokens?**
A: No. CROWDS tokens do not currently exist. Any CROWDS balances in GLX platform are UI mockups for demonstration purposes only.

**Q: When will CROWDS launch?**
A: Realistic timeline is 18-24 months from January 2026, assuming adequate funding and team resources. This includes economic validation, development, audits, and testing phases.

**Q: What makes CROWDS different from other stablecoins?**
A: The **design** introduces novel concepts (contextual fungibility, multi-dimensional value, crisis-specific adaptation). These mechanisms remain **theoretical** until implemented and tested.

**Q: Is ERC-2048 an official Ethereum standard?**
A: No. ERC-2048 is proposed in CROWDS documentation but has not been submitted to the Ethereum community as an official EIP (Ethereum Improvement Proposal).

**Q: How can I get involved?**
A: Review the [Design Phase Status Report](CROWDS_DESIGN_PHASE_STATUS.md) and [Documentation Index](CROWDS_DOCUMENTATION_INDEX.md). Contact GLX Systems Network development team for collaboration opportunities.
